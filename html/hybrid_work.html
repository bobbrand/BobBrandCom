Hybrid Work

I was once leading a software team at a startup and one of the developers approached me about working from home. I joked back that he could work from home any day...after 6pm when he returned from the office.

The hybrid work model that we all inherited from the COVID pandemic appears to be here to stay. While personally, like Mark Zuckerberg, I find hybrid working to be very conducive to my productivity and well-being, I have found that it brings a lot of challenges as a manager. What are some things we can do to keep it effective?
